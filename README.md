## üìÇ How to Add Music to Naa Paata

Naa Paata is a local music player. It does not stream music.
Songs must be added manually using the Files app.

### Step 1: Download Music
Download audio files such as `.mp3` or `.m4a` from any browser or cloud storage.

### Step 2: Open Files App
Open the **Files** app on your iPhone and go to **On My iPhone**.

### Step 3: Find Naa Paata Folder
Inside **On My iPhone**, locate the folder named:

Naa Paata

(This folder is created automatically by the app.)

### Step 4: Move Music Files
Move or copy your downloaded music files into the Music Folder which is inside the **Naa Paata** app folder.

Supported formats:
- mp3
- m4a
- wav (if supported)

### Step 5: Open the App
Launch **Naa Paata**(if Opened, close the app and reopen).
Your songs will automatically sync and appear under Songs, Albums and Playlists.

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Important Notes
- Naa Paata plays only locally stored files
- Internet is not required after adding music
